Climate Impact Snapshot App

Climate change impacts vary significantly throughout India, reflecting the country's diverse climate, topography, and ecology. This app is a great starting point if you want to get a quick overview of climate impacts in your region. The Incline Climate Impact Snapshot App provides climate projections for temperature and precipitation. The Incline Climate Impact Snapshot App is designed to be straightforward and accessible for most users.
