About Us

Incline:

Incline offers a way to explore peer-reviewed data showing how climate change might affect regions across India at both the state and local levels. We provide this data through downloads and visualizations to support your research, outreach, and adaptation planning needs. Incline is now managed by HMPACT Lab researchers under the guidance of Dr. Vivek Gupta


Our mission:

Our mission is to make data portraying climate change in India more accessible and actionable for a broad audience, with an emphasis on energy sector stakeholders and local governments. We do this by:

• Developing tools that allow users to explore and download high-quality, peer-reviewed climate data from India’s scientific and research communities. Incline provides insights from India’s climate change assessments, featuring datasets on soil moisture, droughts, streamflow, and extreme events.

• Creating user-friendly tools and content to help users comprehend climate data through features like tooltips, a Glossary, and the Incline Blog. Our Get Started guide offers best practices for working with climate projections. We facilitate the sharing of climate data charts and tables with stakeholders and offer customization options to meet specific sector requirements in our advanced tools.

• Engaging with our users through workshops, webinars, and stakeholder outreach to gather feedback and improve our offerings.
